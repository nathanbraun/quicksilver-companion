Daniel Waterhouse is named.

**Daniel Waterhouse** — Stephenson's own annotation: "Waterhouse is a reasonably common English name. The Waterhouses, both here and in *Cryptonomicon*, are totally fictitious and bear no relationship whatsoever to any real people named Waterhouse anywhere in the world." He adds that Daniel shares "one or two things in common with a John Wickins who was [Newton](/topic/isaac-newton)'s roommate at Cambridge, but beyond that he is completely fictitious." The real Wickins served as Newton's chamber-fellow at Trinity for twenty years, assisting with experiments and copying manuscripts — a thankless role that Stephenson expands into a full life.
