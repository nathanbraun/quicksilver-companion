Enoch and young Daniel travel together; an Armenian connection is mentioned.

**"Fellow Armenian"** — A reference to Armenian steganographic or cryptographic methods. Armenians in the 17th century maintained extensive trade networks stretching from Isfahan to Amsterdam to Manila, and their merchant diaspora used sophisticated codes to protect commercial intelligence. Whether Stephenson is referencing a specific historical cipher system or inventing one for the novel is deliberately ambiguous — fitting for a book obsessed with the boundary between codes and languages.
