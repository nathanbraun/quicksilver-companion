Daniel's institution is mentioned.

**Massachusetts Bay Colony Institute of Technologickal Arts** — Stephenson's own annotation: "This is totally fictitious. Nothing like it ever existed. It is one of the 'hypotheses' upon which the 'romance' is founded." The joke, of course, is that its name abbreviates to something very close to MIT — which wasn't founded until 1861. One wiki contributor imagined future archaeologists discovering Dr. Waterhouse's cabin ruins near MIT's Infinite Corridor. The anachronism is deliberate: Stephenson is suggesting that the impulse behind MIT — systematizing practical knowledge, the same impulse that drove the [Royal Society](/topic/royal-society) — has older roots than we think.
