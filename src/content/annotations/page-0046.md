Enoch describes his vision to a skeptical Daniel.

**"Campus of domed pavilions"** — Enoch's description of what he imagines will someday stand in Massachusetts — a campus devoted to [natural philosophy](/topic/natural-philosophy) and the technologickal arts. Daniel is skeptical. The wiki notes this as Stephenson's MIT joke: the Massachusetts Bay Colony Institute of Technologickal Arts (MBCITA), which Daniel will later help found, is a direct ancestor of MIT. The real MIT campus on the Charles River features a famous great dome (Building 10), completed in 1916, that would fit Enoch's prophecy nicely. The joke works because Enoch, who seems to exist outside normal time, is describing a real place that won't exist for another 250 years.
